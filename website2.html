ABOUT US

"The Free Student Union (FSU) is a student organization that works to promote and protect the rights and welfare of students.We have been instrumental in raising awareness about issues affecting students, such as the quality of education, affordability, and accessibility. We have also been involved in organizing cultural and extracurricular activities, fostering a sense of unity among students, and promoting social and academic engagement."
